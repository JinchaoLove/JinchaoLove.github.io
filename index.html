Short Bio

Jinchao Li is a PhD candidate at the Human-Computer Communications Laboratory (HCCL) in The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), advised by Prof. Helen Meng. Before joining CUHK, he got a B.S. in Acoustics from Nanjing University in 2019. His research focuses on human-centred AI in healthcare, speech and language, such as intelligent emotion/disease recognition and speech enhancement recently.
